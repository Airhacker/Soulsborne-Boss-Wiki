Sekiro

Sekiro is set in a re-imagined Japan during the late 16th century Sengoku period, where warlord Isshin Ashina has staged a coup and seized control of the land of Ashina. During this time, a nameless orphan is adopted by the wandering shinobi named Ukonzaemon Usui, known to many as "Owl," who named the boy "Wolf" and trained him in the ways of the shinobi.
